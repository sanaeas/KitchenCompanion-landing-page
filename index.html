About

KitchenCompanion was born out of a shared love for cooking and the desire to simplify the culinary journey. The idea was sparked during a cooking session, where the frustration of not having the right ingredients turned into inspiration for a solution. This project is a personal endeavor to make cooking enjoyable for everyone, regardless of their skill level or available ingredients.

The journey began at Holberton School, where we were challenged to create a web development project. Beyond meeting the academic requirements, KitchenCompanion became a passion project, blending technology with the art of cooking.

This project serves as a Portfolio Project for Holberton School, showcasing our skills and creativity in web development.
